Welcome to the Open Quiz!

Open-Quiz is a web platform for producing online quizzes. It supports regulations of What Where When, Jeopardy! and Trivia Quizzes with free form of answers. Questions may contain images. Integration with Mixlr is implemented. There are no limitations on the number of participants!

The service is completely free for commercial and noncommercial use.

Five steps to produce your quiz:

  1. Login
  2. Setup Quiz
  3. Prepare Questions Package
  4. Publish Registration Link
  5. Go Live!
